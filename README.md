### Tracking

The **PiYak** app operates in two distinct modes‚Äî**Counter** (for daily bathroom business) and **Period** (for menstrual flow)‚Äîwhich you can toggle between using the switch located above the calendar. All tracking actions begin by clicking to **select a date** on the calendar.

***

#### üí© Counter Mode (The Daily Dump)

This is the default mode used for tracking your shit everyday.

1.  **Select a Day:** Tap the date on the calendar for the day you want to track.
2.  **Increment:** Click the **`+1`** button to increase the daily count.
3.  **Decrement:** Click the **`-1`** button to correct or decrease the count.
4.  **Visualize:** The calendar day will visually reflect the count, deepening in color intensity (Gold to Deep Brown) as the number increases.

***

#### ü©∏ Period Mode (Aunt Flo's Schedule)

This mode is used for marking your menstrual cycle and viewing predicted dates.

1.  **Setup Prediction (First Use):** Click the **`Settings`** button (next to the mode toggle) and enter your average **Period Length** (days of flow) and **Cycle Length** (total days from the start of one period to the start of the next). Hit **`Save`**.
2.  **Mark Flow:**
    * **Start:** Select the first day of your flow and click the **`Start Period`** button.
    * **Flow:** For any day *during* your flow, click the **`Flow`** button (Flows are automatically filled in between Start day and End day).
    * **End:** Select the last day of your flow and click the **`End Period`** button.
3.  **Correction:** If you need to clear a marking (Start, Flow, or End), select the date and click the **`Clear Marking`** button.
4.  **Prediction:** Once at least two cycles are recorded, the app will automatically display your **Predicted Period** (darker pink), **Fertile Window** (green), and **Predicted Ovulation** (gold) on the calendar.
